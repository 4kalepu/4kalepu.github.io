What is Finance as a Service (FaaS)?

This section introduces the modern financial model we champion. Finance as a Service (FaaS) is a flexible, cost-effective alternative to a traditional in-house finance team. It combines expert professionals, streamlined processes, and cutting-edge technology into a single, subscription-based service. Our Suite of Services

We offer a comprehensive range of financial solutions tailored to your business stage and needs. This section details our core service areas. Click on each service to learn how our expert teams can support your financial operations, from foundational bookkeeping to high-level strategic guidance, ensuring you have the right support at every step of your journey.

Bookkeeping & Controller Level Compliance

We handle all your day-to-day accounting needs, from accounts payable and receivable to month-end close. Our controller-level oversight ensures your books are always accurate and compliant with the latest regulations, giving you a solid foundation for confident financial decision-making.

Strategic Financial Planning & Analysis

We go beyond the numbers to provide forward-looking financial analysis. Our team will help you develop budgets, forecasts, and key performance indicators (KPIs) to track your progress, identify opportunities, and make informed strategic decisions that drive growth.

Complex Accounting Solutions

Our experts can tackle even the most complex accounting challenges. Whether you're dealing with revenue recognition, equity accounting, or preparing for a new standard, we ensure your financial statements are accurate and compliant. We also offer "clean-up" services to get your books in order.

Guidance from Experienced CFOs

Get the benefit of a high-level financial executive without the full-time cost. Our fractional CFOs provide strategic guidance on everything from fundraising and M&A to cash flow management and profitability analysis, acting as a true partner in your success.

Seamless Audit Preparation

We'll help you prepare for a financial audit, ensuring that all your documentation is in order and your team is ready for the process. We work directly with your auditors to make the process as smooth and efficient as possible, saving you time and stress.
